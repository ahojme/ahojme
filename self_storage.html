Self-Storage

Website and isometric illustrations concept • 2020

My roles

Design illustrations and UI, project management.

Goals

To design a user interface of the product's website and develop illustration concept to show the volumes of storage units.

Challenges

  • The illustrations should be in line with visual identity and refer to two other illustrations style currently used.
  • They should consist of elements that can be re-used, to assemble new scenarios efficiently.
  • They should look 3 dimensional and be proportional to each other to show size relations.

Approach

  • Discussed with the team of designers the various needs, possibilities and constraints.
  • Reviewed other illustrations used in the organisation. Searched for inspiration, best practices, design trends, and investigated competitors’ work.
  • Explored the possibilities by working iteratively on concepts, tested possibilities, and considering feedback from the stakeholders.


Results

  • High-fidelity UI mockup.
  • 13 illustrations depicting various storage units.
  • A direction for a new style of isometric illustrations for the organisation.
  • A library of illustration components showing the most used elements for use in future illustrations.

Defining illustrations

The illustrations focus on the size of the room and representative objects that it can accommodate. All units displayed on the website are of the same height to visualise the difference in square footage.
